# 冷邮件模板库

## 模板 1：通用探索性邮件

**使用场景：** 公司没有明确职位，但你感兴趣

**主题行选项：**
- `Quick question about {{company_name}}'s team`
- `{{your_skill}} engineer interested in {{company_name}}`
- `Exploring opportunities at {{company_name}}`

**正文：**
```
Hi {{first_name}},

I'm {{your_name}}, a {{your_title}} with {{years}} years of experience in {{your_domain}}.

I've been following {{company_name}}'s work on {{specific_product_or_news}}, and I'm impressed by {{specific_detail}}. The approach you're taking to {{problem_they_solve}} aligns with what I'm passionate about.

My background includes:
• {{achievement_1}}
• {{achievement_2}}

I'd love to learn more about {{company_name}}'s technical challenges. Would you have 15 minutes for a quick chat?

Best,
{{your_name}}
{{your_linkedin}}

P.S. {{optional_personal_touch}}
```

## 模板 2：针对特定职位

**使用场景：** 看到招聘信息，直接申请

**主题行选项：**
- `{{job_title}} application - {{your_unique_value}}`
- `Re: {{job_title}} role - {{your_name}}`
- `{{job_title}} at {{company_name}} - strong {{skill}} background`

**正文：**
```
Hi {{first_name}},

I saw the {{job_title}} opening at {{company_name}} and believe I'm a strong fit.

Why I'm excited about this role:
{{company_name}}'s {{specific_product/mission}} resonates with me because {{personal_reason}}.

What I bring:
• {{relevant_skill_1}}: {{brief_proof}}
• {{relevant_skill_2}}: {{brief_proof}}
• {{relevant_skill_3}}: {{brief_proof}}

I've attached my resume. Happy to share more about {{specific_project}} if helpful.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best,
{{your_name}}
{{your_portfolio_or_github}}
```

## 模板 3：转介绍请求

**使用场景：** 联系人不是招聘决策者

**主题行：**
- `Quick intro request`
- `Looking to connect with {{company_name}}'s team`

**正文：**
```
Hi {{first_name}},

I'm {{your_name}}, a {{your_title}} exploring opportunities in {{industry}}.

I noticed you're at {{company_name}} - I'm really interested in their work on {{specific_area}}.

Would you be open to a quick 10-minute chat about the team culture? Or if there's someone on the {{target_team}} team you'd recommend I reach out to, I'd appreciate the pointer.

No worries if not - I know everyone's busy.

Thanks,
{{your_name}}
```

## 个性化要点

### 必须个性化的元素
1. **收件人名字** — 绝不用 "Hi there" 或 "Dear Hiring Manager"
2. **公司具体信息** — 产品、新闻、融资、技术栈
3. **为什么是这家公司** — 不能是通用理由

### 研究清单（每封邮件前）
- [ ] 公司最近的新闻/博客文章
- [ ] 他们的产品/技术特点
- [ ] 收件人的 LinkedIn 背景
- [ ] 公司的技术栈（GitHub, StackShare）
- [ ] 任何共同点（学校、前公司、兴趣）

### 主题行最佳实践
- **长度：** 5-8 个词
- **避免：** 全大写、感叹号、"URGENT"、"Amazing opportunity"
- **包含：** 公司名或职位名
- **测试：** 自己会不会打开这封邮件？

```

```

### 完整版
```
Best,
{{name}}
{{title}} | {{specialty}}
{{linkedin_url}} | {{portfolio_url}}
{{phone}} (optional)
```

### 合规签名（如果需要）
```
Best,
{{name}}

---
{{your_address_or_po_box}}
Unsubscribe: Reply with "unsubscribe" to stop receiving emails
```

## 禁忌清单

**不要：**
- 使用 "Dear Sir/Madam"
- 第一封邮件就附简历（除非申请特定职位）
- 写超过 150 词
- 使用通用开头如 "I hope this email finds you well"
- 过度自夸
- 请求太多（第一封邮件只请求一件事）
- 使用垃圾邮件词汇：FREE, URGENT, ACT NOW, !!!
